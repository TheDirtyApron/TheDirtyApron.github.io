Let me tell you something awesome about our company...

A dream & a few dirty aprons

The Dirty Apron began as a University assignment and has quickly grown into a passion project for the partners. This company has allowed us to put into practice our skills of recipe development and marketing to bring you top-notch products. We are four girls who share the same passion for healthy, delicious food. We all study Consumer Science: Food Retail Management at the University of Pretoria. One of our collective dreams was to create an easy way for people to achieve their very best, healthy version of themselves. Being the avid recipe developers that we are, we set out on creating a range of delicious, health conscious products.
With a few dirty aprons later, our protein pancake, protein hot chocolate and bliss ball premixes were born. Our main goal was to create recipes that are quick and easy but still provide the consumer with the feeling of having made the product themselves. Our three versatile products are focused on meeting specific dietary requirements namely; gluten-free, sugar-free, vegan and high in protein. Our premixes are made from minimal, high quality ingredients and are free from funny preservatives. A healthy inside leads to a happy outside!
